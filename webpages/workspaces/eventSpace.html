Event Space

LinkUp's event spaces are fully equipped with modern audio-visual equipment, including large screens or projectors, sound systems, and lighting. They are available in different sizes and configurations, depending on the needs of the customer. They offer flexible booking options, allowing customers to reserve event spaces for a few hours, a full day, or multiple days.

Event spaces are ideal for businesses that require a flexible and professional space to host events and connect with their target audience. They provide businesses with the benefits of a collaborative workspace, such as access to shared resources and networking opportunities, while providing a more exclusive and professional setting for events.

In addition to event spaces, LinkUp's customers have access to shared amenities such as dedicated desks, private offices, printing facilities, and a communal kitchen. They also have the opportunity to collaborate with other professionals in the collaborative workspace environment.
