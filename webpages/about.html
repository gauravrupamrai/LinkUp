Our Story

Expansion driven through Experience

With our extensive understanding of professional networking, LinkUp Workspaces(A LinkedIn subsidiary) aims to provide a vibrant ecosystem where businesses and individuals can connect, collaborate, and grow. By fostering a community that thrives on innovation, we have expanded our reach to over six locations across the Emerald Isle ☘️, offering more than 5 million square feet of state-of-the-art workspaces. Today, we proudly serve over 15,000 members, each contributing to the diverse and thriving community that is LinkUp Workspaces.

Our Infrastructure

Our workspaces are designed to cater to a wide range of businesses, from startups and freelancers to large corporations. Each of our six offices is equipped with cutting-edge facilities and bespoke amenities that cater to the unique needs of our members. Our flexible membership plans allow you to choose the workspace solution that best suits your needs, whether it's a dedicated desk, private office, or collaborative co-working space.

Our Values

At LinkedIn Workspace Solutions, we believe that a workspace should be more than just a place to work. It should be a hub of creativity, inspiration, and collaboration. To that end, we host a variety of events, workshops, and networking sessions aimed at fostering a sense of community and helping our members grow both personally and professionally.

Our Promise

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and the environment. Our eco-friendly workspaces are designed to minimize our carbon footprint while providing a comfortable and productive environment for our members. As we continue to grow, LinkedIn Workspace Solutions remains dedicated to our mission of redefining the modern workspace. We invite you to join our thriving community and experience the difference that a truly innovative workspace solution can make in your professional journey.
